Bergen on a budget

Cafés

Sometimes, people just want a quick bite or even just a place outside from home where they can relax. Those places are Cafés, a place where you can find various people. It is considered as a social environment for some people, where they eat, drink, meeting new people or even find students studying.

Here in Bergen, there are lots of different cafés around. It might be a good idea to have some of these places in mind, considering if you want a change of pace, we recommend these three Cafés which have good products, quite popular while also being reasonably priced.

Recommendations

Dromedar Kaffebar

  • Description: Here you can order from various different coffe types or small food products, as a quick bite on the way.
  • Price range:
    • Food products: 32 NOK - 169 NOK
    • Drinks: 30 NOK - 54 NOK
  • Location: Vestre Torggaten 1, 5015 Bergen, Norway
  • Opening hours:
    • Monday to Friday: 8am - 6pm
    • Saturday and Sunday: 10am - 6pm
  • Phone number: +47 942 72 679
  • Website: dromedar.no
